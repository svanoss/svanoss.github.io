I developed this dashboard during the academic year 2024-25 to
communicate clearly the results of a survey conducted by Penn
States Office of Planning, Assessment, and Institutional
Research and Office for General Education to assess the
experiences of students with their General Education courses.
The data here has been redacted and only images are shown here
to protect the data and privacy of Penn State stakeholders.
Power BI Dashboard Developed to Display Survey Results
The dashboard contains five sections/pages that address
different themes assessed in the survey. Within each page,
viewers can filter to see the responses to each question and the
longitudinal change recorded in the two iterations of this survey in
2019 and 2024.
Viewers can filter by demographic information to
compare and contrast how students of various
identities and academic groups perceive their General
Education experiences.
